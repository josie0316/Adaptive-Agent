Your goal in this task is to serve as many of the orders as you can before each level ends. Each burger scores differently: LettuceBurger is 15 points, BeefBurger is 20 points, and LettuceBeefBurger is 25 points. Note that missing an order or serving the wrong burger will result in a loss of 10 points.
